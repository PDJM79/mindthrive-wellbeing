Why we exist

Financial hardship should never be a barrier to healing.

Across Kent and beyond, thousands of people are navigating the aftermath of trauma without the support they need — not because the expertise doesn't exist, but because access is denied by cost, capacity, and geography.

Current services are overstretched. Waiting lists are long. Private therapy is out of reach for most. The gap between those who can afford support and those who cannot continues to widen.

Mind Thrive Wellbeing CIC exists to close that gap — through trauma-informed, evidence-based programmes delivered to those who need them most, regardless of financial circumstance.

What we deliver

Evidence-based support.
Professionally delivered.

Every service is grounded in clinical evidence, delivered by qualified practitioners, and designed to create lasting outcomes — not just activity.

Trauma-Informed Coaching

1:1 · Groups · ICF-accredited

Individual and group sessions building coping strategies, resilience, and personal goals. Safe, structured, led by accredited coaches with lived understanding.

Therapeutic Interventions

CBT · Brainspotting · Mindfulness

Evidence-based approaches delivered by licensed therapists. Personalised care plans, in-person and remote, matched to the needs of each individual.

Recovery Programmes

Workshops · Peer mentoring · Groups

Structured trauma recovery through workshops, peer mentoring, and support groups — holistic focus on physical, emotional, and social health, informed by the Recover & Thrive model.

CPD Training & Workshops

Accredited · In-house or on-site

Accredited training for professionals and organisations on trauma awareness, resilience-building, and psychological safety. We train the trainers.
